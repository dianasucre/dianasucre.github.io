About our
Diana Sucre

Learn about me, Diana Sucre and everything I have to offer.

 

 

Currently in her senior year pursuing a B.F.A. in Fashion Design and a minor in Business and Entrepreneurship at the Savannah College of Art and Design, the biggest program for fashion design in the United States.

 

The designer was born and raised in the precious land of Venezuela. Within its striking and vibrant colors, its brightness, its pulsating energy, and passionate surroundings, comes all the inspiration; from everyday experiences and extraordinary memories.

Her favorite tool is the mind and the creativity that lives inside of it. Imagination tackles the fear of taking risks. Creativity embraces the power to innovate. Resourcefulness takes her fashion to a whole new place. She aims to create sophisticated but edgy and classy pieces. Her goal is to comprehend and appreciate the human figure so that she can develop the ability to transform it into a remarkable piece of art. Diana focuses on creating beauty rather than self-expression; through every single piece she projects the power and refinement of the modern woman.
